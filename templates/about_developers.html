About the Developers

Just Write was created by three recent high school graduates as their final project for Google's Computer Science Summer Institute (CSSI), an intensive three week programming course in which they learned HTML, CSS, Javascript, and Python. In only four days, they brainstormed, designed, and ultimately created Just Write, a website to encourage innovation and inspire writers.
