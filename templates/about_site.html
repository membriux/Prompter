About the Site

By providing a daily prompt for all users to respond to, Just Write encourages constant innovation and creation, hopefully inspiring users to write about topics they had previously never considered. Users can comment on the writings of other users, offering positive feedback or constructive criticism to help further the writing process.

The vote feature allows users to vote daily for the writings applicable to the prompt of the day that they find particularly imaginative, profound, emotion-inducing, or just all around good. Users may vote only once for each writing, but can vote for many writings each day. The writing that receives the most votes by the end of the day is then featured on the home page the following day.
