About the Site

By providing a daily prompt for users to respond to, Just Write encourages constant innovation and imagination, inspiring users to write about topics they had previously never considered.

Create:

On the create page, you can respond to the prompt of the day by writing a story of your own. The practice of responding daily to a prompt allows you to exercise your creative cerebral while developing your writing skills. On the individual writing pages, you can comment on the writings of other users, offering positive feedback or constructive criticism to help further the creative process all while building a literary network.

Explore:

By navigating to the “All Prompts” page using the menu bar at the top of each page, you can view an archive of all existing prompts. Clicking on a prompt directs you to a page listing all of the writings applicable to the prompt, and clicking on a writing allows you to view the entire writing and offer comments to the author.
You can also explore other creative approaches to the same or previous prompts by navigating and reading through other authors’ stories by clicking on the name of the author. These features allow you to connect with other users across the globe.

Vote:

The vote feature allows you to vote daily for the writings applicable to the prompt of the day that you find particularly imaginative, profound, emotion-inducing, or just all around good. You may vote only once for each writing, but can vote for many writings each day. The writing that receives the most votes by the end of the day is then featured on the home page the following day.
